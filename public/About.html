Prince George's County Food Inspection Database

There is currently no way for people to see what restaurants, food establishments, and areas are compliant and not compliant with Prince George's County Food Inspections.Our application will highlight areas of potential risk and show non-compliant food establishments in an intuitive way to users.

Our project group created a program that maps restaurants in Prince George's County that are non-compliant with county food inspections.

Our stakeholders include Prince George's County Residents, UMD students, local restaurant managers, and the HHS.

The technology stack that we used includes Node.js, HTML/CSS, and the Leaflet JavaScript API.
